How will Studustry help you?


Studustry provides a platform for industries and organizations to engage
with students and colleges through structured project or research work.

Studustry makes campus engagement accountable for industries and adds
value to campus engagement by propogating continuous relationship with
students.

What is the process?



Step 1: Sign up and verify


Create your company account and submit
documents to verify identity.


Step 2: Publish project


Add project hosts and fill project
details for students to view and enroll


Step 3: Execute


Work with students to manage the project
deadlines, deliverables and provide
feedback.


Step 4: Hire


Make recommendations and hire students
who deliver quality on the project.
